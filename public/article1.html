How SkysenseAI Works

Published: Today • Author: SkysenseAI Team

SkysenseAI combines real-time weather data, open-source environmental models, and an integrated chatbot to help aviation users, telescope owners, and technical professionals make better decisions.

It connects to WeatherAPI for fast, accurate current weather — including wind, clouds, visibility, and air quality. For deeper forecasting, SkysenseAI also uses Open-Meteo, which provides open-source global models for temperature, wind, cloud cover, and short-term predictions.

For location-based insights, SkysenseAI integrates OpenStreetMap, allowing users to visualize weather and conditions over airports, dark-sky locations, cities, and observation sites.

The system is supported by a built-in AI assistant designed to help with aviation safety checks, telescope planning, scientific questions, and professional tasks — making SkysenseAI more than just a weather tool.
