Why SkysenseAI Was Made

Published: Today • Author: SkysenseAI Team

SkysenseAI originally started as a simple school project created by a group of five students. What began as a basic idea quickly grew into something much bigger.

The project was meant to explore weather APIs, AI chatbots, and basic data visualization — but as the team worked on it, they realized its potential to help real users in aviation, astronomy, and technical fields.

Instead of keeping it as a private school assignment, the team decided to continue building it, improve the design, add more features, and eventually make it public for everyone to use.

SkysenseAI is now a small but growing platform built from curiosity, teamwork, and the desire to make something useful that people can actually rely on.
